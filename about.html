About · NYC, since 2023

Built in New York. For businesses that don't get to clock out.

Nexus AI Horizon is a small, deliberate studio in lower Manhattan. We build AI voice and SMS systems for four industries — and only four. We turn down the rest.

Our position

Specialists, not generalists.

The AI industry is selling horizontal platforms to vertical businesses. It rarely works. A plumbing company doesn't need a "general purpose AI assistant." It needs a voice that knows what to do when a basement is flooding at 2am.

We made a bet: pick four industries we deeply understand, build production-grade systems for them, and refuse everything else. Three years in, the bet is paying off.
