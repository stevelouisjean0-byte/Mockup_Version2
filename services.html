The platform

Four systems. One operating layer for your business.

AI voice agents, SMS workflows, lead follow-up, and internal automation — deployed together so the call you capture at midnight is also the booked job, the dispatched tech, and the next-day SMS.

Service · 01

AI voice agents.

A custom-trained voice that picks up on the first ring, in your brand's tone, and handles the call end to end. It books, qualifies, escalates, or schedules a callback — without sounding like a chatbot.

  • Custom voice and tone trained on your brand brief
  • Trained on your services, pricing, service area, and rules
  • Symptom-based triage and escalation logic
  • Hand-off to a human the moment it should
  • Full transcript + summary written into your CRM
Service · 02

SMS workflows.

The follow-through your team doesn't have time for. Instant confirmations, pre-job reminders, post-job reviews, and re-engagement sequences — written for your industry, tuned for your customers.

  • Instant confirmation with ETA, tech name, or estimate
  • Multi-touch follow-up sequences that end cleanly
  • Photo intake and document collection over SMS
  • Two-way conversation handed off to humans when needed
  • Compliance-aware: TCPA, opt-out, retention
Service · 03

Lead follow-up automation.

The lead that doesn't book today might book next month — if anyone follows up. Nexus runs structured follow-up sequences across SMS and email, scoring intent and surfacing the leads worth a real call.

  • Multi-channel sequences across SMS and email
  • Intent scoring based on engagement and response
  • Auto-pause when a lead books, opts out, or replies
  • Clean weekly digest of leads worth a call
  • A/B testing built in, no marketing seat required
Service · 04

Internal workflow automation.

The unglamorous work that eats your team alive: intake-to-CRM, dispatch-to-tech, job-to-invoice. We connect the systems you already pay for so your people stop playing copy-paste.

  • Two-way sync across CRM, dispatch, and accounting
  • Auto-generated work orders, invoices, and follow-ups
  • Vendor coordination and document handoff
  • Internal SMS / Slack alerts for the moments that matter
  • Built on your stack — no rip-and-replace
