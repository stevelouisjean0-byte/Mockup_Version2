The problem

Your best people are stuck on the phones.

Maintenance requests, tour bookings, package questions, lease inquiries — they all come through one number, and they all sound urgent. By the time your team gets to the qualified renter, they've already booked elsewhere.

  • 40% of property manager time goes to first-touch calls.
  • Lease leads cool off in under 4 hours. Yours sit overnight.
  • After-hours maintenance routes to a tired on-call — or nowhere.
  • Multilingual buildings have monolingual phone lines.
How Nexus solves it

A front desk that never goes home, in your tenants' language.

Nexus answers your line, opens maintenance tickets with photo intake, books tours into your leasing agent's calendar, and routes the rest where it needs to go. Your office gets quiet. Your numbers get better.

  • Maintenance triage with SMS photo upload and severity tag
  • Lease tour booking synced to leasing agent calendars
  • English, Spanish, Mandarin, Russian — switched mid-call
  • Vendor coordination handoff with auto-generated work orders
